BLOCK CASCADE!

Color Crush Match Puzzle Game

A fresh take on the classic block puzzle game — with a colorful twist. Place blocks on an 8×8 grid, clear rows and columns, and trigger chain reactions by matching colors. Simple to learn, endlessly challenging to master.

Features

🎨

Color Match

Connect 3+ same-colored blocks to trigger bonus clears beyond standard line clearing.

⚡

Chain Reactions

Cascading combos multiply your score. The longer the chain, the bigger the blast!

🧩

16 Unique Pieces

Polyomino shapes that keep gameplay fresh and strategic with every round.

↩️

Undo System

Reverse your last move when you need a second chance to plan better.

🏆

Leaderboard

Compete with players worldwide and climb the global rankings.

🌙

Dark & Light Mode

Play in the style that suits you with beautifully crafted themes.

🌍

Offline Play
